About

PhD student at the UCL Institute of Education, interested in education policy, privatisation of education and global policy mobility. Currently researching the work of new philanthropy in education policymaking and governance in Brazil. Prior to the PhD, completed a BA and Master in Education at the State University of Campinas, Brazil.
